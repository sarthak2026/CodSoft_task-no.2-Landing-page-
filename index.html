Growing and managing mango trees require attention to several key factors to ensure healthy growth and optimal fruit production.Mango trees thrive in tropical and subtropical climates. They require plenty of sunlight and warm temperatures to flourish. Ensure you're planting them in an area where they can receive full sun for most of the day.

VARIETIES

Dasehri

Dasehri mangoes are a popular variety of mangoes cultivated mainly in the northern regions of India, particularly in the states of Uttar Pradesh and Bihar. They have a refreshing taste that is mildly aromatic.

Safeda

Safeda mangoes are primarily enjoyed fresh as a dessert or snack. They are also used to make juices, smoothies, jams, and other mango-based products.

Kesar

Kesar mangoes are a popular variety of mangoes primarily cultivated in the state of Gujarat, India. Kesar mangoes are renowned for their unique flavor profile, which is sweet, rich, and aromatic.

Chaunsa

Chaunsa mangoes are a premium variety of mangoes Chaunsa mangoes are prized for their sweet, rich flavor with a perfect balance of sweetness and acidity.

Totapuri

Totapuri mangoes have a pleasant aroma, it is generally milder compared to some other mango varieties like Alphonso or Chaunsa.

Alphonso

Alphonso mangoes are primarily grown in India, particularly in the region of Maharashtra. They're often considered one of the best-tasting mango varieties.
